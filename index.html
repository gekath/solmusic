Connecting our community through free and accessible music education.


Sol Music is a volunteer-run organization offering weekly music classes to children.

Our mission is to provide performance opportunities for children that may not have access to extracurricular music education.

Classes

Classes take place every Friday from 3:30-4:45 PM at Ryerson Community School. Sol Music operates in accordance with the Toronto School Board (TDSB) School Year Calendar, from September to June. There are no lessons on TDSB approved holidays and PA days.

Curriculum

Sol Music class sizes range from 2-4 students of a comparable music level and age group. Practical and theoretical resources are available for teachers to use, but we also encourage teachers to create and implement their own curriculum based on their studentsâ€™ needs.
