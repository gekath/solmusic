Who We Are

History

Sol Music was founded in 2010 as a non-profit, volunteer-run organization offering weekly music classes to children 6 to 10 years old. Students taking classes also have the opportunity to perform music arranged for the ensemble. Currently, the instruments taught include the guitar, the keyboard, and the violin. Sol Music is a recognized campus group based at the University of Toronto Mississauga.

Objectives

Our objectives are:

  • to provide children with free music lessons and the opportunity to perform in the Sol Music ensemble
  • to foster self-confidence, creativity, discipline, teamwork, and community leadership in children through music-making
  • to promote the arts within the community
  • to encourage leadership and volunteerism by recruiting young musicians as both teachers and mentors to the children
