Join Our Team!

As Sol Music is run entirely by volunteers, there are many volunteer opportunities available that match different interests and skills. Volunteering at Sol Music involves hands-on work and provides a truly rewarding experience. Whether it is through teaching a class, holding a fundraising event, or launching a marketing campaign, volunteers develop enhanced leadership and interpersonal skills, growing personally and professionally.

Opportunities

Volunteer opportunities at Sol Music are open to all members of the public. All applicants must commit to at least half a school year (September to December or January to April). Experience is an asset but is not required as long as applicants have a great attitude and are willing to learn.
